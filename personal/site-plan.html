Overview

Purpose

The website purpose is share with the audience all kind of cakes, pies, sweets and all other things
they can buy here. Also, they will be able to see all kind of flavors and talk to us about special requests they have.
Also, they will find special food for people with allergies or health problems that can't find this kind of things.

Audience

our target audience will be broad. Aiming that we want to reach everyone who likes sweets.
But with a happy theme, we want to focus on families, children and also people who have a hard time
finding sweets that they could eat without worrying about their allergies or health problems.

WELCOME TO FOREVER CANDY
Opening hours of Monday to Saturday from 12PM to 8PM
Our joy is to be able to give everyone the pleasure of participating in magical moments with their friends and family, having fun at a birthday party, a special event, a wedding and so many other opportunities. And all of this is only complete when everyone can eat that delicious piece of cake. That's what we do, we give everyone the opportunity to have a cake without milk or without gluten. Whatever makes you sick, you don't need to worry. More Than Just A Cake
Every turn of our candy pans is made thinking about the moment when you take that big bite and show a wonderful smile. We are open waiting for you to make all your wishes. For a cupcake or a family-sized dessert to take home and eat later.
