HOW TO ORDER

To secure your order, order with at least 5 days in advance

1 - Choose the product and think about the flavors, design or special ingridients to be included. If you are allergic to any ingredient, be sure to let us know.;

2 - Choose the approximate date and time you would like to have your order in hand;

3 - Select if you will pick up your order or opt for delivery;

4 - Choose the form of payment;

5 - After decide this you can send a message or call.
